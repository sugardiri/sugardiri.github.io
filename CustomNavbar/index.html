Nagoya Sushi Restaurant

About us

Welcome to Nagoya Sushi Restaurant! We focus on providing healthy, fresh, and natural dishes to satisfy the modern customer’s tastes and needs. Nagoya Sushi Restaurant was born in 2008, welcoming guests to enjoy Japanese cuisine and the freshest sushi in town. With over a decade of operating in Tallahassee, we offer you warm hospitality and unparalleled service. We strive to provide a vibrant and charming experience every time you visit us.
