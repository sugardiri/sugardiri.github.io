Chapter: Prologue 0

“Ahhhhhhhhhhhhhhh!!!!”

ARA~! There it is again, another mommy’s scream. I can hear it, every day, all the time. I’m not sure what daddy’s doing in the other room. But it smells so rotten.

It’s so stinky and I feel the back of my throat try to control the urge to throw up. But I’m a good girl, so I keep my thoughts inside my head. Just like daddy asked. I won’t question a single thing, because bad girls get haunted by that scary voice. Bad girls are not me, I am good.

Unlike those baaaad mommies! I am good!

……...Hmm...but I wonder….sometimes I hear laughter, sometimes I hear strange sounds I don’t understand. I think daddy’s happy….? Is he playing with the mommies….?

Should I be happy for him…..? I wonder what games they’re playing…? ……………………….I want to know…….but I can’t see…..

What is daddy doing in the other room to the mommy?? Why is daddy always hiding from me…..? If I wasn’t blind…. would I understand? Why does he ignore me, to go play with those stup--nice mommies….? I wonder why? Is it because I’m broken….?

I’m broken, right……? …………………… No……………...

Come to think about it…. daddy says I’m smarter than other girls, he says I’m special and that I’m gifted. I wonder what that means? Nyasha, do you think I’m special too...?

I hold my beloved Nyasha close to my chest and hear her cheerful voice giggle.

I want to giggle too, but I can’t. The stench of rotten meat is so strong that if I speak, I will lose control of myself and throw up my dinner.

Daddy doesn’t like that. He already struggles enough to find me food. If I lose my dinner, he will be mad and I will be bad. Bad girls don’t deserve to live. At least, that’s what I hear him say to those mommies.

Whatever that means. I think it's bad?

What does it mean to live?

Hey, Nyasha….

Nyasha…?

………...

I heard you giggle, my Nyasha. You giggled because you know I’m special, right? I know I’m very special. That’s what daddy tells me, so it must be true. The absolute truth. Even if I can’t see, it doesn’t matter. That’s why you smile for me. That’s why you’re always close to me. That’s why you giggle and play for me…..That's Nyasha.

But I’m tired of waiting….

I want to know what daddy is doing. I want to know what he’s hiding from me. I want to know why this weird rotten smell daddy describes as blood makes me sick.

I just want to know, why would he rather play with those mommies than me? What do they have that I don’t? I thought I was special? I thought I was gifted? I thought I was -one of a kind-? I walk and I walk down the stinky hallway. Slowly, and steadily. Silently. I don’t want daddy to hear. I don’t want him to know that I’m sneaking in on him. I hold Nyasha close to my chest, as I always do. I want her as close to my heart as I can.

The closer I walk, the more I can hear it. The voice of the mommies screaming and the sound of my own daddy laughing. I’m so happy that he’s happy. I want to know why? I want to join in on the fun. I want to be special, too. Not that I’m not already special.

*Creeeeeeek* I open the door, and I smile wide. I want to surprise my daddy with my presence. But oddly enough….

Daddy is so entertained with the mommy, that he fails to notice me… Feeling powerless, I just listen to daddy talking to the mommy….

I can hear my daddy’s excited laughter echoing through the entire room and hallway. The stinky smell is stronger than my own room and I can hear what I think is the sound of rain dripping down from the ceiling.

Daddy: Oh, my beautiful Candelaria! I love you, my lady!

-*COUGH* Y-you w-won’t g-get away with this, Marcello….-

Marcello: What are you talking about my lady? I already got what I wanted from you. Why don’t you just shut up and bleed more for me, hmmm~?

-*COUGH* Y-you c-crazy b-bastard...-

Marcello: My lady! Hush! Corpses don’t speak!

I hear my daddy grab something metallic from a table and next thing I know, I hear the sounds of flesh being stabbed, and bloody screams from this Candelaria mommy.

I think daddy grabbed a knife and it’s hurting her. Why is daddy doing that? Why is he laughing while he stabs her? I don’t understand. Is it that fun? To stab someone? Is it funny?

-Daddy…...?-

I call out to him and I hear daddy drop his knife. I take this opportunity to crawl towards that fun toy and grab it with my own hands. It’s kind of sticky, but that’s fine. I smile once more, hoping that this time he’ll notice me. -Daddy, I want to play with you too…?-

I’m so proud of myself. I finally told daddy. Now we can play. But it’s strange. He’s so quiet and I don’t understand why? I hear his footsteps get closer. He leans down and snatches the knife away from my hand and I hear a loud bang, almost as if his fist hit the wall beside me. Even then, he doesn’t speak to me. Why? Why don’t daddy talk to me!? W-why d-don’t he want to play!?

Marcello: *Sniff* D-don’t j-judge me, C-candy…

Candy:.........?

Marcello: *Sniff* D-damn it! DAMN IT! DAMN IT!

Candy: ...Daddy…?

Marcello: *Sniff* Sorry, sorry, sorry, sorry, sorry!!!!!!!!!!

Before I can say anything else, I feel his hands gripping my neck. I kick my legs and flail my arms around, searching for Nyasha, my only saviour. But she’s not here. I’m burning up inside as I try to gasp for air and it hurts. I can’t breathe.

………………

I’m not sure where I am, but my body hurts so bad and I hear a loud -beep beep- close to my head. It’s so annoying, but it doesn’t stink. There’s someone playing with my hair. That person has soft hands. They smell so nice. Like flowers?

I hear a woman’s voice whisper sweetly..

- Good morning, Candice. My name’s Maure Zuniga.-
